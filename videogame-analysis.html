1. Project Context

The main objective was to identify commercial success patterns to optimize the next advertising campaign for the online store "Ice". Interestingly, I found that the PS3 is the most globally balanced platform with a 21.7% market share, while the X360 dominates North America but performs poorly in Japan with only a 1.79% share.
